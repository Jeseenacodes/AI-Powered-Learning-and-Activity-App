# 🌙 AI-Powered Eid Learning & Card Creator App

An interactive children's app built using AI-assisted vibe coding to help kids learn about Eid while creating personalized greeting cards and enjoying creative activities.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project involved building an interactive children’s app using AI-assisted vibe coding with Lovable. The goal was to help children learn about Eid while creating personalized greeting cards and engaging in creative activities like coloring pages.

The app was designed to make it easy for kids to explore the meaning of Eid while allowing families to share personalized greetings with relatives.

---

## ❗ Problem

Many children celebrate Eid without fully understanding its meaning or traditions. At the same time, families often want simple and creative ways for kids to share Eid greetings with relatives. Traditional greeting cards are limited, and younger children benefit more from **interactive and creative experiences**.

---

## 💡 Solution

Using AI-assisted development, we created a simple app where children can:

- Learn about the significance of Eid  
- Create personalized Eid greeting cards  
- Explore creative coloring pages  
- Share their creations with friends and family  

The app focuses on **simplicity, creativity, and accessibility for younger users**.
